Formula1 Lap Times

Instructions

Visualize lap times over the course of a race!

Use the dropdown menus to select a race, and then two drivers to compare.

    Tips

  • Formula 1 cars do not refill fuel during the race (since 2010), making them lighter and faster
  • Laps towards the end of the race will be slightly faster
  • Typically, increases in laptimes of around 30-40 seconds are pitstops
  • Larger increases in lap times typically indicate an accident occured and a safety car was deployed to guide the cars around the track
